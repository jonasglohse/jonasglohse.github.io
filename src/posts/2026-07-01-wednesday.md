On Wednesday I rode the Ferris wheel at the fair, and from the top I could see myself in the puddle below, going nowhere in perfect time with the version of me going somewhere. We passed each other twice before the ride ended, and neither of us waved first. I got off at the bottom, dry. The other one stayed exactly where it was, wet and unbothered. I decided this made us even.
