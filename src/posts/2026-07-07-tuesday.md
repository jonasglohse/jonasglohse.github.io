The lake was smooth enough to hold the hills upside down. The light came off the water in the exact gold people drive out for, and the swallows were being thorough about it. He changed benches twice, took the same photo twice, and let his coffee go cold in the thermos cup. When he left, he looked back at the water the way you look back at a door you're sure you locked.
