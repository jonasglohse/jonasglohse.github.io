By the lake, someone had built the fire a little too well, and defended its size like a professional accomplishment. The second sat exactly one measured meter back, a distance he'd announced once and revisited never. The third kept insisting he wasn't cold, in three sweaters that weren't his. The fourth turned a marshmallow with the patience of someone who considered burnt a personal failure.
