Four of them sat down for coffee, though only one had actually ordered it, and even she asked for hers "as light as reasonably possible." The second wanted a definition of reasonably, in the tone of someone building a case. The third had switched to water without announcing it, and drank it with the gravity of a man defending a decision. The fourth said nothing, stirred a coffee that wasn't his, and by any visible measure was having the best morning of the four.
