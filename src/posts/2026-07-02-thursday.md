I wrote down everything I had to do today, and by the ninth item the handwriting had stopped looking like my own. The list kept going past the edge of the page, onto the table, onto the back of my hand. Somewhere around "reply to the email" the words lost their shape and became a smell instead — ink, and underneath it something like rain on hot pavement. I looked down expecting a list and found a single sentence in a language I don't speak, which nonetheless made itself understood. I did not get anything done, and felt, for the first time all week, entirely caught up.
