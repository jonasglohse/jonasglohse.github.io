The story about the ferry got told again and landed the way it always had, except she checked the other two before laughing. He paid. She tallied it anyway. The third argued the tally wasn't necessary and lost, like always. Only the drinks were different: the one who used to finish hers first left half a glass standing, and nobody reached for it.
