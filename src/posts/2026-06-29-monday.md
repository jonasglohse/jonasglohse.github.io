At the bakery I told the woman behind the counter that I would have the usual, which I have never ordered before and have never been asked about. She handed me something that may have been what I would order if I were someone who ordered things. I thanked her as if I meant it. On the walk back the bread was warm and the sky was the kind of blue that makes people say things to strangers, so I walked quickly.
